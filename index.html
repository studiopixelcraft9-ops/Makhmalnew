About MAKHMAL

MAKHMAL is a premier Pakistani contemporary label that aims at reinterpreting South Asian heritage and artisanship through a modern lens. Our collections reflect a harmonious blend of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary design sensibilities.

Each piece is thoughtfully designed and meticulously crafted, celebrating the rich textile heritage of Pakistan while appealing to modern aesthetics.
